Big Data

Businesses today operate in a “24x7 experience world” and organizations must reinvent themselves through strong data foundations to stay relevant in this age of digital disruption. With data converging into the enterprises from various forms of structured as well as unstructured; internal as well as external data sources, it has become all the more difficult for them to not only maintain stability but also to adopt to change.

CNET helps effectively manage the 3Vs of data – Velocity, Variety and Volume. We have the knowledge & experience with relevant business use cases, technology solutions and the right data management tools to enable enterprises make Big Decisions from the Big Data.

We provide the following solutions with robust methodologies:
  • Advisory Services – CNET’s experienced advisors make platform assessments and Big Data fitment exercises to recommend best-fits
  • Blueprint – CNET’s data scientists clearly understand the client data patterns and come up with a strategic blueprint to help solve the Big Business Problems
  • Platform migrations to Big Data using mix of open source and traditional DBs and tools
  • Execute Big Data Business Use Case PoCs(proof of concept) in premise or inside lab
  • Create Big Data innovation Labs for the clients to work on new technologies and developments in big data and tools assessments
  • Data Visualization and Analytics – CNET partners with the best-in-class analytical tools in the market and helps visualize data in an understandable form to the business user who is more than often, the sponsor for such projects
  • CNET can implement NoSQL solutions over the cloud which can become a table stake for SMBs
  • Staff Augmentation with best-of-breed talent for in-house developments and projects

CNET’s recommends open source technologies for implementing Big Data Solutions for a quick RoI. We always extend a production parallel model to ensure Business as Usual (BAU) and do not shy away from proposing PoCs / pilot projects; because as we say in the analytics world, seeing is believing.

Business Intelligence and Visualization

With everything around us becoming digital leading to a mass explosion of data, transforming the organizational Decision Support Systems into predictive and prescriptive analytical engines becomes a top priority for the Chief Digital Officer.

CNET helps organizations transform, from relying on IT-led systems of records, to adopt business-driven, self-serviced reporting tools which allow the free flow of data to enable real-time decision making. We have managed to transform boardroom decisions from spreadsheet reports to advanced visualizations, answering more questions, uncovering insights by thinking critically with data.

CNET has proven capability to provide solutions on best-in-class BI & visualization COTS products like SAP, Qlik, Tableau, SAS, Oracle, Microsoft and AWS.

With the help of in-memory, cloud based BI and data discovery tools, CNET enables mobility access for users who move from device to device on the run, enabling quick decision making.

CNET also helps drive analytics directly in clusters leveraging the speed and agility of SQL query engines like Spark and Presto using Hadoop.

Organizations leverage CNET’s BI consulting services to optimize the investments made in the current tools and also assess the best platforms for increased effectiveness in decision making. We help enterprises assess, synergize, create architectural blueprints and implement BI roadmaps.
