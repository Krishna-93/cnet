About CNET

CNET Global Solutions Inc, is an innovative, global IT services and solutions provider headquartered in Dallas, USA. With over a decade of experience in delivering digital transformation and value beyond customer expectations, it partners in customer success across the digital value chain. Our Passion for innovation and Commitment to customer success is what differentiates us. CNET enables its clients to outperform their peers by optimizing their existing operations through legacy transformation; and help them invest in automation through innovation to optimize revenue generation by achieving faster time to market. CNET provides consulting, technology and outsourcing services through its Centers of Excellence and delivery centers spread across USA, Canada and India. Our best-in- class services include Application Transformation, IT service Management, Digital Assurance, Enterprise Solutions and Mobility solutions. CNET offers robust solutions across all major industry verticals through Business Process Management, Big Data, BI & Visualization and next-gen Cloud Solutions. CNET is a Certified Minority Owned Business and is a Texas HUB Certified Organization.
