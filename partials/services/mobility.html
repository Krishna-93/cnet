Mobility Solutions


In this digital era, it is imperative for businesses to stay mobile-enabled to stay relevant, not only to its employees but also to the new age “millennial” customer. CNET ‘s service portfolio for creating a mobile enterprise includes:

Mobility Strategy & Consulting – CNET helps organizations create and/or accelerate mobility blueprint with its expert advisory team which has the access to latest technology platforms, and architectures to accommodate future business requirements.

Mobile Application & Delivery Services – CNET has proven experience on mobile application development and maintenance with a strong UI/UX design team to create a unique experience with native, cross-platform as well as hybrid mobile applications.

Managed Mobility Services – Organizations can accelerate mobility deployments through various hybrid cloud solutions and also manage & monitor the security, back-up and archival tasks. Our comprehensive knowledge of known security threats allows enterprises to create a secure mobile eco-system within the purview of its governance and security policies.

Mobility Assurance – CNET’s Mobility Testing Center of Excellence (TCoE) assures organizations to thrive with constant technology upgrades and deploy new applications quickly reducing time-to-market. Our services include Test Consultancy, Core Applications Testing, Functional Testing, UAT, and Regression Testing, Performance Testing and Test Automation.
